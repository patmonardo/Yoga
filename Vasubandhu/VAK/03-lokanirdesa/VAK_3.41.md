# VAK_3b — VAkK_3.41

## Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

निर्वृत्तिश्च इह पुष्ट्यर्थमाश्रयाश्रितयोर्द्वयम् ।
द्वयमन्यभवाक्षेपनिवृत्त्यर्थ यथाक्रमम् ॥ VAkK_3.41 ॥

## Sanskrit — IAST

nirvṛttiś ca iha puṣṭyartham āśrayāśritayor dvayam /
dvayam anyabhavākṣepanivṛttyartha yathākramam // VAkK_3.41 //

## Source Caution

The source reads:

```text
nirvṛttiśca iha puṣṭyarthamāśrayāśritayordvayam /
dvayamanyabhavākṣepanivṛttyartha yathākramam // VAkK_3.41 //
```

This verse assigns the nutriments by function. The exact pairings should remain provisional until controlled by the Bhāṣya.

## Grammar

```text
nirvṛttiḥ ca iha puṣṭi-artham
    = here, for production and nourishment

āśraya-āśritayoḥ dvayam
    = a pair concerning support and supported

dvayam anya-bhava-ākṣepa-nivṛtti-artha
    = a pair for the projection and cessation of another existence

yathā-kramam
    = respectively / in due order
```

## Literal Translation

Here, for production and nourishment, there is a pair concerning support and supported; another pair is for the projection and cessation of another existence, respectively.

## Philosophical Translation

The nutriments are functionally assigned. Some serve the production and nourishment of the support-supported complex; others serve the projection and cessation of another existence. The four nutriments are therefore not merely listed; they are differentiated by what they do in the continuity of conditioned existence.

## Doctrinal Determination

VAkK_3.40 named the four nutriments:

```text
kavaḍīkāra āhāra
sparśa āhāra
cetanā āhāra
vijñāna āhāra
```

VAkK_3.41 assigns these nutriments by function.

```text
production
nourishment
support / supported relation
projection of another existence
cessation / stopping
```

The key point is that nutriment is not a substance-list. It is a functional system of maintenance and continuity.

## Logical Determination

This verse confirms the active-modeling vocabulary.

```text
āhāra
    = sustaining feature
```

But sustaining is internally differentiated:

```text
producing
nourishing
supporting
projecting another existence
ceasing / stopping
```

Thus nutriment is a feature-set of the conditioned wheel.

```text
conditioned existence
    persists
    grows
    is supported
    projects another becoming
    ceases in ordered ways
```

## Interpretive Note

This is an important continuation of the move from being to essential function.

The four nutriments are not just four things. They are marks/features of how the living conditioned series maintains itself and passes into another existence.

```text
listed nutriment
    → functional nutriment
        → feature of continuity
```

## Organon Interpretation

Light contact point:

```text

```

VAkK_3.41 makes this explicit for nutriment. The wheel is fed by functions, not merely by objects.

```text

    and what lets it project another existence
```
