# VAK_3.77 — World-Measure: Higher Rūpa-Deva Body-Measures

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```sanskrit
अर्धार्धवृद्धिरूर्ध्वं तु परीत्ताभेभ्य आश्रयः ।
द्विगुणद्विगुणा हित्वानभ्रकेभ्यस्त्रियोजनम् ॥ VAkK_3.77 ॥
```

## 3. Source Caution

The source reading is:

```iast
ardhārdhavṛddhi ūrdhva tu parīttābhebhya āśrayaḥ /
dviguṇadviguṇā hitvānabhrakebhya striyojanam // VAkK_3.77 //
```

Sandhi has been lightly separated:

```iast
ardha-ardha-vṛddhiḥ ūrdhvam tu parīttābhebhyaḥ āśrayaḥ /
dvi-guṇa-dvi-guṇāḥ hitvā anabhrakebhyaḥ tri-yojanam //
```

The text continues the measure of the `āśraya`, the bodily support, among higher rūpa-devas. The exact distribution across the rūpa-deva classes should be checked against the bhāṣya. In this first pass, the main structural point is preserved: after the initial rūpa-deva measure, the higher body-supports increase by ordered fractional and doubling patterns.

## 6. Literal Translation

Above, the bodily support increases by halves from the Parīttābhas. Leaving aside the Anabhrakas, it is doubled and doubled; three yojanas [are specified there].

## 7. Philosophical Translation

The higher rūpa-deva body-supports are measured by ordered increases. From the Parīttābha level upward, the measure increases by halves; beyond the Anabhraka point, the scale proceeds by successive doubling, with a three-yojana measure marked in the sequence.

## 9. Doctrinal Determination

VAkK_3.75 measured human bodies by continent. VAkK_3.76 measured kāma-deva bodies and began the rūpa-deva body scale. VAkK_3.77 completes this immediate body-measure sequence by continuing the higher rūpa-deva scale.

```text
human bodies
    → continent measure

kāma-deva bodies
    → quarter-increase

rūpa-deva bodies
    → half-yojana beginning
    → half-by-half increase
    → successive doubling
```

This gives a clean closing point for the space/body-measure block before the text turns directly toward lifespan and time in VAkK_3.78.

## 10. Logical Determination

This verse extends proportional embodiment into higher levels.

```text
higher realm
    → higher body-support
        → ordered increase-pattern
            → fractional increase
            → doubling increase
```

The important term is `āśraya`: the body is treated as support or basis. The Kośa is measuring not only visible form but the support through which a being occupies its world-level.

## 11. Interpretive Note

This is a good stopping point for the present thread. The spatial block has moved from cosmic locations to world-systems, from world-systems to inhabitants, and from inhabitants to bodily support.

```text
mountains / rivers / terraces / cities
    → world-systems
        → continents
            → human bodies
                → deva bodies

```

The next verse, VAkK_3.78, begins with lifespan and therefore shifts the dominant register from spatial magnitude to temporal duration.

## 12. Organon Interpretation

Light contact point:

```text
Embodiment is scaled as the support appropriate to a world-level.
```

For this pass, we simply observe the Kośa’s presentation. Loka is not merely location. It gives the field in which beings have bodies, and those bodies have determinate measures according to the world-level they inhabit.
