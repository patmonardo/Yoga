# VAK_3.86 — Measure: Atomic Scale from Paramāṇu to Finger-Joint

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```sanskrit
लोहाप्शशाविगोच्छिद्ररजोलिक्षास्तदुद्भवाः ।
यवस्तथाङ्गुलीपर्व ज्ञेयं सप्तगुणोत्तरम् ॥ VAkK_3.86 ॥
```

## 2. Sanskrit — IAST


## 3. Source Caution

The source reading is:

```iast
lohāpśaśā vigocchidrarajolikṣāstadudbhavāḥ /
yavastathāṅgulīparva jñeyaṃ saptaguṇottaram // VAkK_3.86 //
```

This verse continues the atomic sequence begun at the end of VAkK_3.85: `paramāṇur aṇus tathā`. The compact compound in the first pāda is difficult and should be checked against the bhāṣya and printed editions. It is provisionally segmented as a sequence of increasingly larger dust/particle measures:

```iast
loha-ap-śaśa-avi-go-cchidra-rajaḥ-likṣāḥ tad-udbhavāḥ /
yavaḥ tathā aṅgulī-parva jñeyam sapta-guṇa-uttaram //
```

The main structural point is secure: each subsequent measure is known as seven times the preceding one.

## 6. Literal Translation

The iron-[dust], water-[dust], rabbit-[dust], sheep-[dust], cow-[dust], aperture-dust, and likṣā arise from those. Likewise the barley grain and finger-joint should be known as successively sevenfold greater.

## 7. Philosophical Translation

The atomic scale now expands from the ultimate atom into perceptible and bodily measures. Each level arises from the preceding one by a sevenfold increase: from subtle particle-measures through dust, nit, barley grain, and finally the finger-joint.

## 9. Doctrinal Determination

VAkK_3.85 gave the minimal limits:

```text
rūpa
    → paramāṇu

nāma
    → akṣara

time
    → kṣaṇa
```

VAkK_3.86 continues the rūpa side by constructing a scale of material magnitude.

```text
paramāṇu
    → aṇu
        → dust-particle measures
            → likṣā
                → yava
                    → aṅgulī-parva
```

Each step is governed by a sevenfold relation.

## 10. Logical Determination

The verse gives a rule of material scaling.

```text
minimal material unit
    → sevenfold aggregation
        → next material measure
            → repeated sequence
```

This is not a vague movement from small to large. It is a determinate measure-series.

## 11. Interpretive Note

After kalpas and mahākalpas, the Kośa now gives the opposite pole: minute rūpa-measure. The world is measured not only by aeons and lifespans, but by atom, dust, grain, and finger-joint.

```text
macro-measure
    → kalpa

micro-measure
    → paramāṇu

intermediate embodied measure
    → yava / aṅgulī-parva
```

This is crucial for the Loka-nirdeśa: world is not merely an imagined cosmos. It is a measured domain from atom to aeon.

## 12. Organon Interpretation

Light contact point:

```text
Form becomes world-scale through lawful aggregation.
```

For this pass, we keep close to the Kośa. The important move is that rūpa is not just given as gross body or location. It is analytically generated upward from minimal material determination by a fixed ratio.
