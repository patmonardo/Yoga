# Organon Project: Transcendental Reconstruction of the Abhidharma

## Working Research Program (July 2026)

---

# 1. Fundamental Hypothesis

The Sanskrit Abhidharma, especially the *Abhidharmakośabhāṣya* (VAK), is not merely:

* a psychology,
* a phenomenology,
* a cosmology,
* or a taxonomy of mental events.

Rather, it is fundamentally:

> **a transcendental analysis of the conditions of possible facticity.**

In this sense, the Sarvāstivāda project stands in structural affinity with:

* Kant's Transcendental Logic,
* Fichte's Wissenschaftslehre,
* Hegel's Science of Logic,
* and the broader Vedic inquiry into the conditions of cognition.

---

# 2. The Organon Hypothesis

The Abhidharma is approached as an:

> **Examination of the Instrument** (*Organon*).

The central question becomes:

> What must the instrument of possible experience be?

rather than:

> What objects exist?

This transforms Abhidharma from ontology into transcendental methodology.

---

# 3. Working Principle of Interpretation

We suspend:

* later scholastic polemics,
* sectarian disputes,
* excessive commentarial expansion,
* merely psychological interpretations.

Instead we seek:

> the architectural concepts underlying the system.

---

# 4. Reconstruction of Indriya Theory

## Standard interpretation

Indriyas are:

* senses,
* faculties,
* organs,
* powers.

## Organon interpretation

Indriyas are:

> operators of transcendental cognition.

They implement the lawful conditions under which experience becomes possible.

---

# 5. Sattvic Theory of the Indriyas

Working hypothesis:

> All indriyas are fundamentally sāttvika.

This includes:

### Rational indriyas

* buddhi
* ahaṃkāra
* manas
* the higher internal determinations

and also:

### Embodied indriyas

* sensory faculties
* karmendriyas
* embodied cognitive functions

The later reduction of embodied faculties to merely empirical status is regarded as a secondary doctrinal development.

# 7. The Copernican Theory of Facticity

Kant:

> Objects conform to cognition.

Fichte:

> Facticity itself is generated.

Hegel:

> The generation of facticity is itself rational.

The Organon hypothesis:

> Abhidharma investigates the conditions under which facticity appears.
