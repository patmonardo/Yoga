# VAK_3.88 — Measure: Yojana and the Micro-Time Sequence

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```sanskrit
तेऽष्टौ योजनमित्याहुर्विंशं क्षणशतं पुनः ।
तत्क्षणस्ते पुनः षष्टिर्लवस्त्रिंशद्गुणोत्तराः ॥ VAkK_3.88 ॥
```

## 2. Sanskrit — IAST

```iast
te 'ṣṭau yojanam ity āhur viṃśaṃ kṣaṇaśataṃ punaḥ /
tatkṣaṇaḥ te punaḥ ṣaṣṭir lavaḥ triṃśad-guṇottarāḥ // VAkK_3.88 //
```

## 3. Source Caution

The source reading is:

```iast
te 'ṣṭau yojanamityāhuḥ viṃśaṃ kṣaṇaśataṃ punaḥ /
tatkṣaṇaḥ te punaḥ ṣaṣṭirlavaḥ triṃśad guṇottarāḥ // VAkK_3.88 //
```

Sandhi has been lightly separated:

```iast
te aṣṭau yojanam iti āhuḥ viṃśam kṣaṇa-śatam punaḥ /
tat-kṣaṇaḥ te punaḥ ṣaṣṭiḥ lavaḥ triṃśat-guṇa-uttarāḥ //
```

The first pāda is clear: eight of the preceding unit, the krośa, make a yojana. The second half begins a time-unit sequence. The transmitted wording is compressed and should be checked against the bhāṣya. Provisionally, the verse is taken to introduce a progression from kṣaṇa to tatkṣaṇa, from tatkṣaṇa to lava, and then toward larger time-units continued in VAkK_3.89.

## 6. Literal Translation

Eight of those are called a yojana. Again, a hundred kṣaṇas with the twentieth [measure] make a tatkṣaṇa; sixty of those again make a lava; the following [units] increase by thirtyfold.

## 7. Philosophical Translation

The spatial measure ladder reaches the yojana: eight krośas make one yojana. The text then turns immediately to the ladder of time-units, moving from kṣaṇa toward tatkṣaṇa and lava, with further units increasing by a larger ratio and continuing into the next verse.

## 9. Doctrinal Determination

VAkK_3.87 completed the linear bodily-distance ladder up to krośa. VAkK_3.88 first completes that ladder by defining the yojana, then opens the time-unit ladder.

```text
krośa
    × 8
        = yojana
```

Then:

```text
kṣaṇa
    → tatkṣaṇa
        → lava
            → larger time-units continued in VAkK_3.89
```

The same analytic habit applies to space and time: both are constructed as ordered measure-series.

## 10. Logical Determination

This verse makes the hinge from spatial extension to temporal extension.

```text
space-series
    → krośa
        → yojana

time-series
    → kṣaṇa
        → tatkṣaṇa
            → lava
```

The world is not merely located in space and time. Space and time themselves are made intelligible through measure-ladders.

## 11. Interpretive Note

VAkK_3.85 stated the three limits:

```text
rūpa-limit
    → paramāṇu

nāma-limit
    → akṣara

adhvan/time-limit
    → kṣaṇa
```

VAkK_3.86–3.87 developed the rūpa/space side from atom to yojana. VAkK_3.88 now begins to develop the adhvan/time side from kṣaṇa upward.

```text
atom → yojana
moment → day / month / year / kalpa
```

This is why the verse is structurally important: it mirrors the material measure-series with a temporal measure-series.

## 12. Organon Interpretation

Light contact point:

```text
The world-field is calculable because both space and time are scaled from minima.
```

For this pass, we stay with the Kośa. The Buddha’s analytic mastery would include not only seeing atoms, but also knowing the layered construction of temporal units from the moment upward.
