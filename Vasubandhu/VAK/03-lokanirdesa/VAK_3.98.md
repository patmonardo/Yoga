# VAK_3.98 — Loka Genealogy: Storage, Field-Protector, and Lifespan Decline

```sanskrit
आलस्यात्संनिधिं कृत्वा साग्रहैः क्षेत्रपो भृतः ।
ततः कर्मपथाधिक्यादपहरासे दशायुषः ॥ VAkK_3.98 ॥
```

## 6. Literal Translation

Because of laziness, having made stores, with claims they maintained a field-protector. Thereafter, because karmapathas predominate, there is decline down to ten-year lifespan.

## 7. Philosophical Translation

The genealogy of the present human world continues: laziness gives rise to storage; storage gives rise to claims; claims require a protector of fields. Thereafter, as karmapathas predominate, lifespan declines until it reaches the ten-year condition.

## 10. Logical Determination

```text
accumulation
    → possession
        → protection
            → authority
```

The social order arises from a degeneration of relation: beings begin to store, claim, and require protection.

## 11. Interpretive Note

This verse prepares Karma-nirdeśa directly. Loka is not merely spatial cosmology; it is a world-history shaped by karmapathas. The decline of lifespan is tied to the predominance of action-paths.

## 12. Organon Interpretation

```text
World-order becomes social order when stored goods require protected fields.
```

The present human world is genealogically derived from taste, storage, claim, field, authority, and karmic decline.
