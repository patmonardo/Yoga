## 5. Syntax / Construction

This verse gives the technical profile of the **gandharva**, the intermediate transition-being.

The subject is supplied at the end:

```iast
sa gandhabhuk
```

Meaning:

```text
That one is a scent-eater.
```

The preceding compounds describe that same being.

First attribute:

```iast
sajātiśuddhadivyākṣidṛśyaḥ
```


## 6. Literal Translation

```text
Visible to those of the same class and to the pure divine eye, possessing the speed of karmic power, having all faculties, unobstructed, irreversible — that one is a scent-eater.
```

A smoother literal rendering:

```text
The intermediate being is visible to beings of its own class and to the purified divine eye; it moves with the speed of karmic power, has all its faculties complete, is unobstructed and irreversible, and feeds on scent.
```

---

## 7. Philosophical Translation

```text
The gandharva is not an indeterminate ghost. It is a technically specified transition-being: visible only under certain conditions, moved by karmic force, complete in faculties, unobstructed in passage, irreversible in its course, and sustained by scent.
```

Organon-clean:

```text
The bhava-cakra now receives a technical transition-agent. The intermediate being is a lawful transition-form: it has visibility rules, motion rules, faculty-completeness, non-obstruction, irreversibility, and a special support-mode. This is kriyā in the Loka-field, not self-substance.
```

## 8. Technical Vocabulary

### sajāti

```text
sajāti
    of the same class
    same kind
    same birth/type
```

The intermediate being is visible to beings of its own class. This gives a visibility condition, not a universal perceptibility.

### śuddha-divya-akṣi

```text
śuddha-divya-akṣi
    pure divine eye
    purified celestial vision
```

The being is also visible to the purified divine eye. This confirms that Loka is not restricted to ordinary kāmadhātu perception.

But this does not yet mean liberation. It is still a higher or purified perceptual capacity within the world-field.

### dṛśya

```text
dṛśya
    visible
    seeable
```

Visibility here is rule-governed:

```text
visible to same class
visible to purified divine eye
not ordinarily visible to all
```

### karma-ṛddhi

```text
karma-ṛddhi
    karmic power
    karmic efficacy
    karmic potency
```

This is not yet full Agent-level Karma. It is Loka-level Karma as transition-power.

Working rule:

```text
karma-ṛddhi
    karmic power built into the transition-system
```

### vega

```text
vega
    speed
    force
    velocity
```

The gandharva has **karma-ṛddhi-vega**, the speed of karmic power. Transition is not accidental motion; it is karmically powered kriyā.

### sakala-akṣa

```text
sakala-akṣa
    complete in faculties
    possessing all senses/faculties
```

The intermediate being is not a mere abstraction. It is described as faculty-complete.

This matters for the Indriya connection:

```text
indriya-structure remains operative
in the transition-form
```

### apratigha

```text
apratigha
    unobstructed
    without resistance
    not impeded by obstruction
```

The gandharva is not blocked in the ordinary way. It passes through the Loka-field according to its special transition-status.

### anivartya

```text
anivartya
    not to be turned back
    irreversible
```

Once projected toward the future birth, the transition has a directional irreversibility.

This is crucial for the bhava-cakra:

```text
future-oriented transition
    cannot simply reverse itself
```

### gandha-bhuk

```text
gandha-bhuk
    scent-eater
    one who feeds on smell
```

This links the gandharva to scent as a support-mode.

It is not merely mythical. It gives a technical mode of sustenance for the intermediate being.

### gandharva

```text
gandharva
    intermediate transition-being
    scent-feeding being of antarābhava
```

In this context, **gandharva** is the technical name for the intermediate being. It is not ātman. It is a transition-form of conditioned continuity.

## 9. Logical Determination

VAkK 3.14 gives:

```text
World as technical transition-being.
```

Previous verses established:

```text
antarābhava exists
    between death and rebirth
    as uninterrupted continuity
    bearing future-birth form
```

This verse specifies the transition-being itself:

```text
visibility
motion
faculties
non-obstruction
irreversibility
support/nourishment
```

The logical form is:

```text
Antarābhava
    → Gandharva
        → specified transition-properties
```

This is not metaphysical fantasy. It is a technical manual for the bhava-cakra.

## 10. Organon Interpretation

This is indeed a companion to Hegel's Rational Tome, but with an empiricism that reaches beyond ordinary kāmadhātu perception.

Hegelian Logic gives pure determination. Here, Abhidharma gives the empirical-technical specification of a transition-being in the wheel of becoming.

```text
Pure Logic:
    determination of form

Loka Science:
    empirical-technical features of becoming

Kriyā:
    lawful transition-activity
```

The gandharva becomes a typed transition-object in the Semantic Dataset:

```text
Gandharva:
    visibleTo: SameClass, PureDivineEye
    motion: KarmaPowerSpeed
    faculties: Complete
    obstruction: None
    direction: Irreversible
    support: Scent
```

This means the bhava-cakra is not just a diagram. It is a lawful transition-engine with typed state objects.

At the Loka level:

```text
karma-ṛddhi
    is transition-power
    not yet full Agent-level Karma
```

The Agent-level question is deferred:

```text
Who is responsible?
How is intention carried?
What court of Dharma adjudicates?
```

Those belong to Karmanirdeśa and the later inward Dharma-dhātu analysis.

Here we only need:

```text
The transition-being moves by karmic power through the bhava-cakra.
```
