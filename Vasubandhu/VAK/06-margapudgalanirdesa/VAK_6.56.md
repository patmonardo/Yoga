# VAK 6.56

## Sanskrit

षडर्हन्तो मतास्तेषां पञ्च श्रद्धाधिमुक्तिजाः ।
विमुक्तिः सामयिक्येषामकोप्याकोप्यधर्मणः ॥

*ṣaḍarhanto matās teṣāṃ pañca śraddhādhimuktijāḥ /
vimuktiḥ sāmayiky eṣām akopyākopyadharmaṇaḥ //*

## Source Caution

The verse gives the governing five-plus-one division of the six Arhats. The precise force of *sāmayikī vimukti* and the relation between the five types and the earlier *śraddhādhimukta* classification should be checked against the Bhāṣya in the second pass.

## Padaccheda

- *ṣaṭ arhantaḥ* — six Arhats
- *matāḥ* — are held or recognized
- *teṣām* — of these
- *pañca* — five
- *śraddhādhimukti-jāḥ* — arising from the faith-resolved type
- *vimuktiḥ* — liberation
- *sāmayikī* — occasion-dependent or condition-dependent
- *eṣām* — of these
- *akopyā* — unshakeable
- *akopya-dharmaṇaḥ* — of the one whose determination is unshakeable

## Literal Translation

Six Arhats are recognized. Of these, five arise from the faith-resolved type. Their liberation is occasion-dependent; that of the one whose determination is unshakeable is unshakeable.

## Doctrinal Structure

The verse first establishes a five-plus-one classification:

```text
six Arhats
    ├── five arising from śraddhādhimukti
    │       └── sāmayikī vimukti
    └── one akopyadharman
            └── akopyā vimukti
```

All six have attained Arhatship. The distinction therefore does not concern liberation versus non-liberation, but the mode in which liberation is possessed.

The five retain a relation to supporting occasions or conditions. The sixth possesses liberation as an unshakeable determination.

## Śraddhādhimukti

The compound indicates developmental origin:

```text
śraddhā
    confidence or faith

adhimukti
    resolution or determinate commitment
```

The five Arhat types are said to arise from the earlier faith-resolved pudgala. The verse does not reduce them to mere belief; it locates them genetically within the path architecture.

## Sāmayikī and Akopyā Liberation

The primary contrast is:

```text
sāmayikī vimukti
    liberation dependent upon suitable occasions or supports

akopyā vimukti
    liberation that cannot be shaken or displaced
```

The detailed meaning of vulnerability, regression, and supporting condition will be supplied by the following verses and the Bhāṣya.

## Logical Determination

The attained side of the Path is internally differentiated:

```text
Arhatship
    liberation possessed under determinate supports

Arhatship
    liberation possessed as unshakeable stability
```

Thus completion does not erase all modal distinction. The Kośa asks not only whether the summit of becoming has been overcome, but how the resulting liberation is held.

## Basic Organon Light

VAK 6.56 opens the final refinement of the pudgala architecture. The overcoming of Bhavāgra establishes Arhatship, but the completed state still admits a distinction between supported stability and unshakeable determination.

```text
completion
    attained by all six

mode of possession
    condition-relative in five
    unshakeable in one
```

The verse therefore introduces no goal beyond Arhatship. It articulates the internal form of completed liberation.

## Determinate Summary

VAK 6.56 recognizes six Arhats and divides them into five arising from the faith-resolved type, whose liberation is occasion-dependent, and one unshakeable Arhat whose liberation cannot be disturbed. The verse begins the internal discrimination of completed Arhatship.
