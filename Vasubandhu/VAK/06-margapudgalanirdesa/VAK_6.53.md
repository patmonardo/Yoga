# VAK 6.53

## Sanskrit

ज्ञानाष्टकस्य लाभोऽथ षोडशाकारभावना ।
लौकिकाप्तं तु मिश्रत्वादनास्रवाप्तिर्धृतेः फलम् ॥

*jñānāṣṭakasya lābho 'tha ṣoḍaśākārabhāvanā /
laukikāptaṃ tu miśratvānāsravāptiḥ dhṛteḥ phalam //*

## Source Caution

The first half clearly completes the five causes begun in VAK 6.52: acquisition of the eight knowledges and cultivation of the sixteen aspects. The second half is compressed; its exact syntax and the sense of *dhṛti* should be revisited in the Bhāṣya pass.

## Padaccheda

- *jñāna-aṣṭakasya lābhaḥ* — acquisition of the eight knowledges
- *atha* — and then
- *ṣoḍaśa-ākāra-bhāvanā* — cultivation of the sixteen aspects
- *laukika-āptam* — what was attained through a worldly path
- *miśratvāt* — because of combination or mixture
- *anāsrava-āptiḥ* — acquisition of the uncontaminated
- *dhṛteḥ phalam* — fruit through retention or possession

## Literal Translation

And there is acquisition of the eight knowledges and cultivation of the sixteen aspects. What was attained through the worldly path, through its combination with uncontaminated acquisition, is retained as fruit.

## Completion of the Five Causes

VAK 6.52 gave:

1. relinquishment of the preceding status,
2. attainment of another path,
3. consolidation of cessations in the fruit.

VAK 6.53 adds:

4. acquisition of the eight knowledges,
5. cultivation of the sixteen aspects.

A principal fruit is therefore not a bare cessation-event. It is a complete path-position in which abandonments, a new path, integrated cessations, articulated knowledge, and cultivated aspects are held together.

## Eight Knowledges and Sixteen Aspects

The eight knowledges articulate the four truths across the relevant domains. The sixteen aspects articulate each truth through four determinations. Liberation therefore culminates not in indeterminate awareness but in a differentiated truth-system.

```text
four truths
    × two domains
        → eight knowledges

four truths
    × four aspects
        → sixteen aspects
```

## Worldly Attainment Re-determined

The second half indicates that cessations previously acquired through worldly dispassion can be retained when the uncontaminated path is subsequently attained. The earlier content is preserved, but its standpoint is superseded and reorganized under the uncontaminated path.

```text
worldly attainment
    preserved as accomplished content

uncontaminated acquisition
    becomes governing determination

integrated result
    belongs to the fruit
```

## Basic Organon Light

The five causes form a complete architecture:

```text
previous form relinquished
    ↓
new mediation attained
    ↓
determinate negations integrated
    ↓
truth-system acquired
    ↓
its aspects cultivated
    ↓
fruit as achieved totality
```

The fruit is neither an isolated event nor a heap of prior attainments. It is a new whole that preserves earlier results while reorganizing them under a higher path-determination.

## Determinate Summary

VAK 6.53 completes the fivefold basis of the four fruits. A fruit includes acquisition of the eight knowledges and cultivation of the sixteen aspects, and it can integrate results formerly reached by worldly dispassion once these are re-determined under uncontaminated acquisition. Fruit is therefore an articulated and cultivated totality of Path.
