## 4. Grammar

### savitarkavicārāḥ

```text
sa-vitarka-vicārāḥ
```

This compound qualifies **pañca vijñānadhātavaḥ**. The prefix **sa-** means "with." The five knowledge-domains are not themselves identical with vitarka and vicāra; they are domains of knowledge accompanied by these determinations.

### pañca vijñānadhātavaḥ

```text
pañca = five
vijñāna-dhātavaḥ = knowledge-domains
```

The five are the five sensory knowledge-domains:

```text
cakṣurvijñānadhātu
śrotravijñānadhātu
ghrāṇavijñānadhātu
jihvāvijñānadhātu
kāyavijñānadhātu
```

They are not "consciousness-elements." In this project vocabulary, **vijñāna** is knowledge and **dhātu** is domain.

### antyāḥ trayaḥ

```text
antyāḥ trayaḥ = the last three
```

In the eighteen-domain ordering, these are:

```text
manodhātu
dharmadhātu
manovijñānadhātu
```

These are the same three that VAK_1.31 singles out as capable of being sāsrava/anāsrava.

### triprakārāḥ

```text
tri-prakārāḥ = threefold, of three modes
```

The last three domains are threefold with respect to vitarka and vicāra. The likely doctrinal modes are:

```text
1. sa-vitarka-sa-vicāra
   with vitarka and with vicāra

2. a-vitarka-vicāra-mātra
   without vitarka, with vicāra only

3. a-vitarka-a-vicāra
   without both vitarka and vicāra
```

This remains marked for bhāṣya review, but it is the working analysis for the Organon file.

### śeṣā ubhayavarjitāḥ

```text
śeṣāḥ = the remaining ones
ubhaya-varjitāḥ = without both
```

The remaining ten domains are the five sense-faculty domains and the five object domains. They are not loci of this double cognitive articulation.

## 5. Literal Translation

> For the five knowledge-domains are with vitarka and vicāra; the last three are threefold; the remaining ones are without both.

## 6. Philosophical Translation

> The five sensory knowledge-domains arise together with vitarka and vicāra. The final triad — mind-domain, dharma-domain, and mental-knowledge-domain — admits a threefold determination with respect to vitarka and vicāra. The remaining domains, being neither active knowledge-domains nor the final mental triad, are free from both.

Hegel-clean working rendering:

> The five sensory knowledge-domains are determinate knowings accompanied by vitarka and vicāra. The final triad has a threefold modal structure. The remaining domains are not loci of this double logical determination.

## 8. Logical Determination

This verse classifies the eighteen domains by relation to a double cognitive-logical determination:

```text
A. Five knowledge-domains
   = with vitarka and vicāra

B. Last three domains
   = threefold with respect to vitarka/vicāra

C. Remaining ten domains
   = without both
```

The verse is therefore not merely an inventory. It introduces a modal partition of the eighteen domains according to their participation in the internal articulation of knowledge.

```text
Determinate sensory knowledge
        ↓
Threefold mental/dharma articulation
        ↓
Non-discursive remainder
```

The triple here is not transcendental. It incarnates a higher Idea. The final three domains are not themselves the transcendental ground; they are the threefold manifestation of a higher cognitive determination.

## 9. Interpretive Note

VAK_1.32 is a decisive bridge between the domain-classification and the later account of differentiation in VAK_1.33.

The five sensory knowledge-domains are not mute empirical receptions. They are already accompanied by **vitarka** and **vicāra**. Thus sensory knowledge is already internally articulated.

The final three domains — **manodhātu**, **dharmadhātu**, and **manovijñānadhātu** — are more plastic. They can appear in three modes:

```text
with both vitarka and vicāra
with vicāra only
with neither vitarka nor vicāra
```

The remaining ten domains are excluded from both. They are support/object domains, not the operative loci of this discursive-logical articulation.

### Organon note on vitarka and vicāra

The inherited monastic renderings of **vitarka** and **vicāra** as merely "applied thought" and "sustained thought" are inadequate for this project, because they flatten their logical function.

Working Organon determination:

```text
vitarka
        = vi-tarka
        = Reason as immediate determination
        = immediate determinations of Being

vicāra
        = essential reflection
        = determination of Being through reflective articulation
```

In this reading, VAK_1.32 belongs to Principle Formation. It classifies the domains according to their participation in the logical articulation of knowledge. This prepares VAK_1.33, where **nirūpaṇa**, **anusmaraṇa**, and **vikalpa** become possible as determinate forms of differentiation.

The developing sequence remains:

```text
vijñāna
    ↓
vitarka / vicāra
    ↓
nirūpaṇa
    ↓
anusmaraṇa
    ↓
vikalpa
    ↓
saṅkalpa
```
