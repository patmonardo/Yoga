## 4. Literal Translation

> Stainless prajñā, together with its attendants, is Abhidharma; and whatever exists for the attainment of that, and whatever treatise there is, because that is substantially included in this, and because this is its support, it is called the Abhidharmakośa.

## 5. Philosophical Translation

> Abhidharma is undefiled knowing, together with the determinations that accompany it. This work is called the Abhidharmakośa because it contains, in meaning, both that Abhidharma and the disciplined science directed toward its attainment, and because it serves as the repository of that science.
