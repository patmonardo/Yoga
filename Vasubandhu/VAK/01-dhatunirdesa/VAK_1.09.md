# VAK_1.9

## 1. Sanskrit (Devanāgarī)

> रूपं पञ्चेन्द्रियाण्यर्थाः पञ्चाविज्ञप्तिरेव च ।
>
> तद्विज्ञानाश्रया रूपप्रसादाश्चक्षुरादयः ॥

## 2. Sanskrit (IAST)

> rūpaṃ pañcendriyāṇyarthāḥ pañcāvijñaptireva ca /
>
> tadvijñānāśrayā rūpaprasādāścakṣurādayaḥ //

## 4. Literal Translation

> Form consists of the five faculties, the five objects, and also non-manifest form. The eye and the other faculties are clear material bases that support their corresponding cognitions.

## 5. Philosophical Translation

> Material determination comprises the five cognitive faculties, the five objective domains, and non-manifest determination. The sensory faculties beginning with vision function as refined material supports for their corresponding modes of cognition.
