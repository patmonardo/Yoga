# VAK_1.7

## 1. Sanskrit (Devanāgarī)

> ते पुनः संस्कृता धर्मा रूपादिस्कन्धपञ्चकम् ।
>
> स एवाध्वा कथावस्तु सनिःसाराः सवस्तुकाः ॥

## 2. Sanskrit (IAST)

> te punaḥ saṃskṛtā dharmā rūpādiskandhapañcakam /
>
> sa evādhvā kathāvastu saniḥsārāḥ savastukāḥ //

## 4. Literal Translation

> These constructed dharmas are the five aggregates beginning with form. They are indeed the temporal course, the object of discourse, possessed of continuity, and possessed of objectivity.

## 5. Philosophical Translation

> Constructed reality consists in the five aggregates. These constitute the field of temporal determination, the domain of possible discourse, the sphere of conditioned continuity, and the domain of determinate objectivity.
