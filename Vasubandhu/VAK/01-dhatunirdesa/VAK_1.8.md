# VAK_1.8

## 1. Sanskrit (Devanāgarī)

> ये सास्रवा उपादानस्कन्धास्ते सरणा अपि ।
>
> दुःखं समुदयो लोको दृष्टिस्थानं भवश्च ते ॥

## 2. Sanskrit (IAST)

## 4. Literal Translation

> Those conditioned-by-outflow dharmas are the aggregates of appropriation. They are also suffering, origination, world, the basis of views, and becoming.

## 5. Philosophical Translation

> The dharmas conditioned by outflow constitute the appropriated aggregates. They thereby define the domain of suffering, the principle of origination, the world as experienced, the ground of determinate viewpoints, and the process of becoming.
