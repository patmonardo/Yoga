# VAK_1.34

## 1. Sanskrit (Devanāgarī)

> सप्त सालम्बनाश्चित्तधातवः अर्धं च धर्मतः ।
>
> नवानुपात्ता ते चाष्टौ शब्दश्चान्ये नव द्विधा ॥

## 2. Sanskrit (IAST)

## 4. Literal Translation

> Seven knowledge-domains possess object-support, and half of the dharma-domain likewise. Nine are unappropriated: the eight and sound. The remaining nine are twofold.

## 5. Philosophical Translation

> Seven knowledge-domains are intrinsically object-directed, and one portion of the dharma-domain likewise possesses intentional objectivity. Nine domains are unappropriated: the eight material domains together with sound. The remaining nine exhibit a twofold determination.

## 8. Interpretive Note

This verse introduces a new principle of classification among the domains: object-supported, appropriated, unappropriated, and twofold. The concern is functional determination rather than ontology.
