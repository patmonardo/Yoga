# VAK_1.22

## Sanskrit (Devanagari)

> स्कन्धेष्वसंस्कृतं नोक्तमर्थायोगात् क्रमः पुनः ।
>
> यथौदारिकसंक्लेशभाजनाद्यर्थधातुतः ॥

## IAST

> skandhesv asamskrtam noktam arthayogat kramah punah /
>
> yathaudarikasamklesabhajanadyarthadhatutah //

## Literal Translation

The unconditioned is not included among the the skandhas because it does not fit their meaning. Further, the order is determined according to grossness, defilement, receptaclehood, meaning, and dhatu.

## Philosophical Interpretation

This verse establishes that the order of exposition in the Abhidharma is itself rational and pedagogical.

Skandha = conditioned principle of manifestation.
Ayatana = mediating connection.
Dhatu = continuity of the principle under law.

The encyclopedia of dharmas is therefore a serial process of learning rather than a static taxonomy.

Dharma(n) -> understanding -> completion -> Dharma(n+1)

## Notes

This supports the interpretation of pratisaṃkhyā-nirodha as a serial process of discriminative learning and completion.
