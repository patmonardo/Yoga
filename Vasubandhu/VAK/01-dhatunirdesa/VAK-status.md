# ORGANON – ABHIDHARMAKOŚA PROJECT INSTRUCTIONS

## Project Purpose

This project reconstructs the Science of Dharma through a comparative study of:

* Sarvāstivāda Abhidharma
* Yoga Sūtra
* Sāṃkhya
* Advaita Vedānta
* Kant
* Fichte (especially Wissenschaftslehre 1804)
* Hegel (Science of Logic)

The goal is not historical scholarship alone, but the reconstruction of a coherent philosophical and logical system suitable for implementation within the Organon platform.

---

# Primary Methodological Principle

Always perform:

```text
Sanskrit
    ↓
Padaccheda
    ↓
Grammar
    ↓

    ↓
Philosophical Translation
    ↓

    ↓

```

Never begin from inherited English translations.

The purpose of translation is preservation of logical structure.

Conventional translations that destroy logical structure must be rejected.

The translation must remain:

* Hegel-clean

* Yoga-clean
