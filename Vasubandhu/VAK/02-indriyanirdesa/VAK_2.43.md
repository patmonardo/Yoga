# VAK_2.43 — Nirodha-Samāpatti Profile

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```text
निरोधाख्या तथैवेयं विहारार्थं भवाग्रजा ।
शुभा द्विवेद्यानियता च आर्यस्य आप्या प्रयोगतः ॥ VAkK_2.43 ॥
```

## 4. Literal Translation

```text
This, called nirodha, is likewise for the sake of abiding and is born from the summit of becoming.
It is wholesome, twofold in result-experience and indeterminate; it is acquired by the noble one through practice.
```

## 5. Philosophical Translation

```text
Nirodha-samāpatti is profiled as a special attainment of cessation.
It serves the purpose of abiding, belongs to the summit of becoming, is wholesome,
has a special result-profile, and is acquired by the noble one through deliberate practice.
```
