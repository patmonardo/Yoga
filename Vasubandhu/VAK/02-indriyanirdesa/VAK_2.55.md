# VAK_2.55 — Temporal Scope of Hetus and the Phala Domain

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```text
सर्वत्रगः सभागश्च द्वयध्वगौ त्र्यध्वगास्त्रयः ।
संस्कृतं सविसंयोग फलं नासंस्कृतस्य ते ॥ VAkK_2.55 ॥
```

## 2. Sanskrit — IAST

```text
sarvatragaḥ sabhāgaś ca dvayadhvagau tryadhvagās trayaḥ /
saṃskṛtaṃ savisaṃyoga phalaṃ nāsaṃskṛtasya te // VAkK_2.55 //
```

## 3. Literal Translation

```text
Sarvatraga and sabhāga operate in two times; three operate in three times.
Their fruit is conditioned and with disconnection; not of the unconditioned.
```

## 4. Philosophical Translation

```text
The hetus are now given temporal scope and result-domain.
Some ground-types operate across two times, others across three.
Their fruits belong to the conditioned domain, with the special case of disconnection-result; they do not produce the unconditioned as ordinary conditioned effects.
```
