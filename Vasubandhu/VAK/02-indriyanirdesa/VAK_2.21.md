## 3. Textual Caution

This verse gives high or maximal faculty-configurations with exclusions. It is technically delicate and should be checked later against the Bhāṣya. The present file records the speculative seed only.

## 4. Literal Translation

```text
One configured with many is configured with nineteen, excluding the stainless.
A two-sexed noble one with passion is configured excluding one sex, the two, and the stainless.
```

## 5. Working Interpretation

VAK_2.21 gives the upper side of the configuration analysis.

```text
ekānnaviṃśati
    = nineteen

amala-varjita
    = excluding the stainless / pure faculties

dviliṅga
    = having two sex-marks / two sexual faculties

ārya
    = noble one

rāgī
    = one with passion / attachment
```

The verse therefore contrasts a high-count configuration with a more qualified noble-but-still-passionate configuration.
