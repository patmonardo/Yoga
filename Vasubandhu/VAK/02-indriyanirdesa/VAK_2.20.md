```text
सर्वाल्पैर्निःशुभोऽष्टाभिर्विन्मनःकायजीवितैः ।
युक्तः बालस्तथारूप्ये उपेक्षायुर्मनःशुभैः ॥ VAkK_2.20 ॥
```

## 3. Textual Caution

This verse concerns lowest or minimal faculty-configurations. The compounds are compressed and the exact scenario-mapping should be checked against the Bhāṣya.

## 4. Literal Translation

```text
With the fewest of all, one without wholesome faculties is configured with eight:
feeling, mind, body, and life. Likewise, an ordinary being in the formless realm is configured with equanimity, life, mind, and the wholesome faculties.
```

## 5. Working Interpretation

The verse appears to define minimal configurations under two conditions:

```text
niḥśubha bāla
    = ordinary/untrained condition lacking wholesome faculties

ārūpya bāla
    = ordinary condition in the formless realm
```

The first minimal configuration is connected with:

```text
vitti / vedanā
manas
kāya
jīvita
```

The formless case is connected with:

```text
upekṣā
āyus / jīvita
manas
śubha faculties
```
