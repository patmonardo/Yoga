## 3. Textual Caution

This verse continues faculty-configuration analysis, now applying it to the realization faculties. The source has a spacing/sandhi irregularity in **ājñājñāte ndriya**, preserved in the source-form line.

## 4. Literal Translation

```text
One configured with the knowing and known faculties is accompanied by eight or eleven.
One configured with the “I shall know” faculty is accompanied by thirteen.
```
