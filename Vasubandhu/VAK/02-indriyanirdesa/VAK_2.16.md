# VAK_2.16 — Gradual Death and Attainment of Final Fruits

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```text
क्रममृत्यौ तु चत्वारि शुभे सर्वत्र पञ्च च ।
नवाप्तिरन्त्यफलयोः सप्ताष्टनवभिर्द्वयोः ॥ VAkK_2.16 ॥
```

## 3. Textual Caution

This verse continues the acquisition/cessation count-analysis and introduces attainment of final fruits. Exact count mapping should be checked later against the Bhāṣya.

## 4. Literal Translation

```text
But in gradual death, four; in the wholesome case, everywhere five.
There is acquisition of nine at the final fruits; of the two, by seven, eight, or nine.
```

## 5. Working Interpretation

The verse contains two themes:

```text
kramamṛtyu
    = gradual death / sequential dying

antya-phala
    = final fruit / final result
```

It therefore connects faculty-counts both to death-process and attainment-process.
