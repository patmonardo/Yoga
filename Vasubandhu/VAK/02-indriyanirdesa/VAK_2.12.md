# VAK_2.12 — Realm Classification of the Faculties

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```text
कामाप्तममलं हित्वा रूपाप्तं स्त्रीपुमिन्द्रिये ।
दुःखे च हित्वा आरूप्याप्तं सुखे चापोह्य रूपि च ॥ VAkK_2.12 ॥
```

## 3. Textual Caution

This verse classifies faculties by realm: desire-realm, form-realm, and formless-realm. The exclusions are compressed and should be checked later against the Bhāṣya. This file preserves the kārikā-first structure.

## 4. Literal Translation

```text
Belonging to the desire-realm, excluding the stainless;
belonging to the form-realm, excluding the female and male faculties and pain;
belonging to the formless-realm, excluding pleasure and the material faculties.
```

## 5. Working Interpretation

The verse sorts faculties by the three realms.

```text
kāmāpta
    = belonging to the desire-realm

rūpāpta
    = belonging to the form-realm

ārūpyāpta
    = belonging to the formless-realm
```

The exclusions reveal that not every faculty operates in every realm.

```text
desire-realm
    excludes the stainless / pure

form-realm
    excludes female and male faculties and pain

formless-realm
    excludes pleasure and material faculties
```

## 7. Logical Determination

The faculties are now classified by realm-location.

```text
indriya
    = governing efficacy
    + realm-condition
```

A faculty is not merely a universal function; it has a determinate range of operation across the three realms.
