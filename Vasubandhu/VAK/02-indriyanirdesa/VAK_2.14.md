# VAK_2.14 — Initial Acquisition of Faculties

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```text
कामेष्वादौ विपाको द्वे लभ्यते नोपपादुकैः ।
तेः षड्वा सप्त वा अष्टौ वा षड् रूपेषु एकमुत्तरे ॥ VAkK_2.14 ॥
```

```text
kāmeṣvādau vipāko dve labhyate nopapādukaiḥ /
```

## 3. Textual Caution

This verse concerns acquisition of faculties at the beginning of existence in different modes of birth and realms. The transmitted source has the form **teḥ**, preserved here as source-form. Count mechanics should be checked against the Bhāṣya.

## 4. Literal Translation

```text
In the desire-realm at the beginning, two resultant faculties are acquired,
not by the spontaneously born. By them, six or seven or eight; six in the form-realms,
one in the higher realm.
```

## 5. Working Interpretation

The verse shifts from classification-status to **acquisition-status**.

```text
labhyate
    = is obtained / acquired

ādau
    = at the beginning

vipāka
    = resultant / karmically matured
```

It tracks what faculties are obtained at the beginning of a life-stream under different conditions.
