# VAK_2.05 — The Functional Extent of the Faculties

## 1. Sanskrit — Devanāgarī

```text
चित्ताश्रयस्तद्विकल्पः स्थितिः संक्लेश एव च ।
संभारो व्यवदानं च यावता तावदिन्द्रियम् ॥ VAkK_2.5 ॥
```

```text
    nominative / accusative singular neuter
    “purification,” “clarification”

    correlative expression
    “as far as ... so far,” “to whatever extent ... to that extent”

```

The last phrase is decisive:

```text
yāvatā tāvat indriyam
```

That is:

```text
There is faculty just to the extent that these functions require governing efficacy.
```

## 5. Recapitulation of VAK_2.01–2.04

VAK_2.05 summarizes the first unit of the Indriyanirdeśa. The twenty-two faculties are not an arbitrary list. They are exactly coextensive with the functional requirements of experience and liberation.

```text
citta-āśraya
    -> six cognitive faculties

    cakṣus
    śrotra
    ghrāṇa
    jihvā
    kāya
    manas

tad-vikalpa
    -> female and male faculties

    strī-indriya
    puruṣa-indriya

sthiti
    -> life faculty

    jīvita-indriya

saṃkleśa
    -> five feeling faculties

    sukha
    duḥkha
    saumanasya
    daurmanasya
    upekṣā

saṃbhāra
    -> five pure / constructive faculties

    śraddhā
    vīrya
    smṛti
    samādhi
    prajñā

vyavadāna
    -> three realization faculties

    ājñāsyāmīndriya
    ājñendriya
    ājñātāvīndriya
```

Thus:

```text
support
    differentiation
        persistence
            entanglement
                path-equipment
                    purification
```

This is the functional arc of the indriya doctrine.

## 6. Literal Translation

```text
Support of mind, its differentiation, persistence,
defilement, equipment, and purification —
as far as these extend, so far there is faculty.
```

A slightly smoother literal:

```text
There is faculty just insofar as there is support of mind,
its differentiation, persistence, defilement, equipment,
and purification.
```

## 7. Philosophical Translation

```text
Faculties are posited exactly to the extent that experience requires governing
powers: to support cognition, differentiate the field, sustain the living series,
bind it through defilement, equip it for the path, and clarify it through purification.
```

## 9. Vikalpa Note

The term **tad-vikalpaḥ** must be handled carefully.

The standing project rule remains:

```text
vikalpa ≠ concept
```

In VAK_2.05, **tad-vikalpa** is best read as:

```text
tad-vikalpa
    =
its differentiation
```

That is, the differentiation of the supported field. In the immediate context of VAK_2.02, this points especially to the differentiation of embodiment into female and male determinations.

```text
strī-indriya
puruṣa-indriya
    =
faculties governing tad-vikalpa,
its differentiation
```

So here **vikalpa** does not mean conceptual thought. It names determinate differentiation within the functional field.

## 10. Saṃbhāra Note

The term **saṃbhāra** is equally important.

Possible translations:

```text
accumulation
equipment
provision
requisite collection
path-resource
```

For VAK_2.05, the working translation is:

```text
saṃbhāra
    =
path-equipment / requisite accumulation
```

The five pure faculties are not themselves final realization. They equip, provision, and prepare the series for purification.

```text
śraddhā
vīrya
smṛti
samādhi
prajñā
    =
saṃbhāra,
path-equipment for purification
```

Thus VAK_2.03 and VAK_2.04 are clarified by VAK_2.05:

```text
VAK_2.03
    saṃbhāra
    path-equipment

VAK_2.04
    vyavadāna
    purification / clarification actualized through realization
```

## 11. Logical Determination

VAK_2.05 gives the formal rule for the twenty-two faculties.

```text
There are indriyas exactly where there is a required functional governing efficacy.
```

The six functions are:

```text
citta-āśraya
    support of cognition

tad-vikalpa
    differentiation of the field

sthiti
    persistence of the living series

saṃkleśa
    entanglement of the series

saṃbhāra
    equipment of the path

vyavadāna
    purification / clarification
```

Therefore:

```text
indriya
    =
sāttvic-rational governing efficacy
required at each functional point
in the Logic of Experience.
```

This verse is not merely enumerative. It is justificatory.

```text
Why twenty-two faculties?

Because experience and liberation require exactly these governing functions:
    support,
    differentiation,
    persistence,
    entanglement,
    path-equipment,
    purification.
```

## 12. Transcendental Placement

VAK_2.05 is the synthetic closure of the first five kārikās.

It states that the indriyas are coextensive with the required conditions of governed experience.

```text
experience must have a support
experience must become differentiated
experience must persist as a series
experience becomes affectively entangled
experience gathers path-equipment
experience becomes purified / clarified
```

This is the arc of conditioned experience from appearance to liberation.

```text
support
    -> differentiation
        -> persistence
            -> entanglement
                -> equipment
                    -> purification
```

The doctrine is transcendental because it identifies the functional conditions under which experience and liberation are possible. But it is not Absolute metaphysics. It belongs to the Logic of Experience.

```text
not a list of empirical organs
but a doctrine of governing powers

not merely psychology
but the functional architecture of experience and release
```

The relation between Chapters 1 and 2 now becomes clear.

```text
Dhātunirdeśa

the domain-structure of determinate law

Indriyanirdeśa
    =
the faculty-structure of governed experience
```

Or:

```text
dhātu
    =
where dharmas belong

indriya
    =
how dharmas operate with governing efficacy
```

And in Kant-clean terms:

```text
Dhātu
    =
Concept / mediacy / domain

Indriya
    =
Intuition / immediacy / faculty

Vijñāna
    =
knowledge-event through their conjunction
```

## 14. Organon Interpretation

VAK_2.05 gives the complete Organon schema of the first unit of Chapter 2.

```text
Indriya as support-driver:
    citta-āśraya

Indriya as differentiation-driver:
    tad-vikalpa

Indriya as persistence-driver:
    sthiti

Indriya as entanglement-driver:
    saṃkleśa

Indriya as path-equipment-driver:
    saṃbhāra

Indriya as purification-driver:
    vyavadāna
```

The chapter-level formula is:

```text
indriya
    =
sāttvic-rational governing efficacy
by which a dhātu becomes available,
differentiated,
sustained,
entangled,
equipped,
and purified
within the conditioned series.
```

This confirms the architectural pair:

```text
VAK_1:
    Dhātu as Concept / mediated domain

VAK_2:
    Indriya as Intuition / immediate governing faculty
```

Thus experience arises through the conjunction of domain and faculty:

```text
Dhātu without Indriya
    =
empty domain-structure

Indriya without Dhātu
    =
blind immediacy

Dhātu + Indriya
    =
structured experience
```
