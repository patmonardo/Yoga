# Technical Analysis of Mantra 2.33

## I. Base Text

### A. Sanskrit
```sanskrit
अथ चेत्त्वमिमं धर्म्यं सङ्ग्रामं न करिष्यसि |
ततः स्वधर्मं कीर्तिं च हित्वा पापमवाप्स्यसि || 33 ||
```

### B. Key Term Analysis
1. धर्म्य (dharmya)
   - Adjectival from धर्म
   - Technical term: "righteous/dharmic"
   - Connected to essential order

2. सङ्ग्राम (saṅgrāma)
   - सम् + ग्राम
   - Technical term for battle
   - Beyond mere fighting

3. स्वधर्म (svadharma)
   - Essential nature/duty
   - Technical philosophical term
   - Original ordering principle

# Technical Analysis of Śaṅkara's Commentary on BG 2.33

## I. Opening Framework

### A. Initial Structure
एवं कर्तव्यताप्राप्तमपि (evaṃ kartavyatāprāptam api)
1. Key Technical Terms:
   - कर्तव्यता (kartavyatā) = duty/obligation
   - प्राप्त (prāpta) = obtained/established
   - Technical compound showing established duty

## II. Analysis of धर्म्य (dharmya)

### A. Śaṅkara's Definition
1. Two-part explanation:
   - धर्मादनपेत (dharmādanapeta) = not separated from dharma
   - विहित (vihita) = prescribed/ordained

2. Technical Precision:
   - धर्मादनपेत shows essential connection
   - विहित shows authoritative status

## III. Battle Analysis

### A. Technical Terms
1. सङ्ग्राम (saṅgrāma)
   - Equated with युद्ध (yuddha)
   - Technical equivalence
   - Essential nature specified

### B. Consequence Structure
1. Triple Loss Framework:
   - स्वधर्म (svadharma) = essential duty
   - कीर्ति (kīrti) = fame/glory
   - Specified as: महादेवादिसमागमनिमित्ता (mahādevādisamāgamanimitta)
     = caused by association with great gods etc.

2. Final Result:
   - केवलं पापम् (kevalaṃ pāpam) = pure/mere sin
   - Technical use of केवल (kevala)
   - Absolute negative result

## IV. Philosophical Structure

### A. Logical Framework
1. Conditional Structure:
   - अथ चेत् (atha cet) = technical conditional
   - ततः (tataḥ) = logical consequence

2. Result Pattern:
   - From established duty (कर्तव्यताप्राप्त)
   - Through non-performance
   - To pure negative (केवलं पापम्)

### B. Technical Vocabulary
1. Key Compounds:
   - कर्तव्यताप्राप्त (kartavyatāprāpta)
   - धर्मादनपेत (dharmādanapeta)
   - महादेवादिसमागमनिमित्त (mahādevādisamāganimittā)

2. Technical Terms:
   - विहित (vihita) = prescribed
   - केवल (kevala) = pure/mere
   - निमित्त (nimitta) = caused by

## V. Commentary Structure

### A. Method of Explanation
1. Initial Framework:
   - Established duty
   - Technical possibility

2. Full Analysis:
   - Nature of dharmic battle
   - Consequence structure
   - Final result

### B. Technical Precision
1. Careful Definitions:
   - धर्म्य explained through धर्मादनपेत
   - कीर्ति specified through देवादिसमागम
   - पाप qualified as केवल

2. Logical Development:
   - From established duty
   - Through non-performance
   - To pure negative result
