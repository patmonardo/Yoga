# Technical Analysis: Bhagavad Gītā 2.33

## I. Mūla Verse and Structure

```sanskrit
अथ चेत्त्वमिमं धर्म्यं सङ्ग्रामं न करिष्यसि |
ततः स्वधर्मं कीर्तिं च हित्वा पापमवाप्स्यसि || 33 ||
```

## II. Śaṅkara's Commentary

```sanskrit
एवं कर्तव्यताप्राप्तमपि यदि त्वं धर्मादनपेतं विहितं धर्म्यं सङ्ग्रामं युद्धं न करिष्यसि । ततः स्वधर्मं क्षात्रं कीर्तिं च महादेवादिसमागमनिमित्तां हित्वा परित्यज्य केवलं पापमवाप्स्यसि ।
```

### A. Bhāṣya Analysis
1. Initial Framework:
   - एवं कर्तव्यताप्राप्तम् (evaṃ kartavyatāprāptam):
      * कर्तव्यता (kartavyatā) = established duty
      * प्राप्त (prāpta) = obtained/reached
      * Technical term for binding obligation

2. धर्म्य Analysis:
   - Double definition:
      * धर्मादनपेत (dharmādanapeta) = not deviating from dharma
      * विहित (vihita) = prescribed/ordained
      * Complete dharmic determination

3. Consequence Structure:
   - Triple loss specified:
      * स्वधर्म क्षात्र (svadharma kṣātra) = essential warrior duty
      * कीर्ति महादेवादिसमागमनिमित्ता (kīrti mahādevādisamāganimittā) = divine-sourced glory
      * केवलं पापम् (kevalaṃ pāpam) = pure negative result

## III. Technical Term Analysis

### A. Conditional Framework
1. अथ चेत् (atha cet):
   - Technical conditional
   - Philosophical hypothesis
   - Critical choice point

2. ततः (tataḥ):
   - Logical consequence
   - Necessary following
   - Essential connection

### B. Action Terms
1. सङ्ग्राम (saṅgrāma):
   - Formation: सम् + ग्राम
   - Śaṅkara's gloss: युद्ध
   - Technical term beyond mere battle

2. हित्वा (hitvā):
   - Formation: √हा + क्त्वा
   - Śaṅkara's gloss: परित्यज्य
   - Complete abandonment

## IV. Triple Loss Analysis

### A. Essential Nature (स्वधर्म)
1. Technical Specification:
   - क्षात्र धर्म
   - Essential nature
   - Original order

2. Loss Implication:
   - Beyond mere duty
   - Essential deviation
   - Original disorder

### B. Divine Glory (कीर्ति)
1. Technical Definition:
   - महादेवादिसमागमनिमित्ता
   - Divine connection
   - Transcendental aspect

2. Loss Structure:
   - Beyond mere fame
   - Divine separation
   - Essential isolation

### C. Pure Negativity (पाप)
1. Technical Qualification:
   - केवल specification
   - Absolute nature
   - Complete deviation

2. Final Result:
   - Beyond mere sin
   - Essential disorder
   - Pure negativity

## V. Connection to Previous Verses
