## Mūla Verse

### Sanskrit
यः सर्वत्रानभिस्नेहस्तत्तत्प्राप्य शुभाशुभम् ।
नाभिनन्दति न द्वेष्टि तस्य प्रज्ञा प्रतिष्ठिता ॥ ५७ ॥

### Transliteration
yaḥ sarvatrānabhisnehas tattat prāpya śubhāśubham |
nābhinandati na dveṣṭi tasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā || 57 ||

### Translation
One who is everywhere without attachment, encountering this or that, be it pleasant or unpleasant,
Neither rejoices nor hates - their wisdom is firmly established.

## Śaṅkara's Commentary

### Sanskrit
यः मुनिः सर्वत्र देहजीवितादिष्वपि अनभिस्नेहः अभिस्नेहवर्जितः तत्तत् प्राप्य शुभं अशुभं वा लब्ध्वा न अभिनन्दति न द्वेष्टि शुभं प्राप्य न तुष्यति न हृष्यति, अशुभं च प्राप्य न द्वेष्टि इत्यर्थः । तस्य एवं हर्षविषादवर्जितस्य विवेकजा प्रज्ञा प्रतिष्ठिता भवति ॥

### Transliteration
yaḥ muniḥ sarvatra dehajīvitādiṣvapi anabhisnehaḥ abhisnehavarjitaḥ tattat prāpya śubhaṃ aśubhaṃ vā labdhvā na abhinandati na dveṣṭi śubhaṃ prāpya na tuṣyati na hṛṣyati, aśubhaṃ ca prāpya na dveṣṭi ityarthaḥ | tasya evaṃ harṣaviṣādavarjitasya vivekajā prajñā pratiṣṭhitā bhavati ||

### Translation
That sage who is everywhere, even regarding body and life, without attachment, free from clinging, obtaining this or that, pleasant or unpleasant, neither rejoices nor hates - obtaining the pleasant, is neither satisfied nor delighted, and obtaining the unpleasant, does not hate - this is the meaning. For such a one who is free from pleasure and depression, wisdom born of discrimination becomes firmly established.

## Technical Analysis

### Key Compound Terms
1. अनभिस्नेह (anabhisneha):
   - अभिस्नेहवर्जित (abhisnehavarjita)
   - Negative compound: "without attachment"
   - Root स्निह् (snih) with अभि prefix

2. शुभाशुभ (śubhāśubha):
   - Dvandva compound
   - शुभ (śubha) - pleasant/good
   - अशुभ (aśubha) - unpleasant/bad

3. हर्षविषादवर्जित (harṣaviṣādavarjita):
   - Triple compound:
   - हर्ष (harṣa) - joy
   - विषाद (viṣāda) - depression
   - वर्जित (varjita) - free from

### Abhidharmic Categories
1. Attachment Types:
   - देह-स्नेह (deha-sneha) - body attachment
   - जीवित-स्नेह (jīvita-sneha) - life attachment
   - सर्वत्र-स्नेह (sarvatra-sneha) - universal attachment

2. Response Pairs:
   - अभिनन्दन/द्वेष (abhinandana/dveṣa)
   - तुष्टि/हृष्टि (tuṣṭi/hṛṣṭi)
   - हर्ष/विषाद (harṣa/viṣāda)

3. विवेकजा प्रज्ञा (vivekajā prajñā):
   - Born of discrimination
   - Established (प्रतिष्ठिता)
   - Complete realization

### Technical Terms
1. मुनि (muni):
   - Sage
   - One of silence
   - Established in wisdom

2. प्रज्ञा-प्रतिष्ठा (prajñā-pratiṣṭhā):
   - Firm establishment
   - In discriminative wisdom
   - Complete realization

### Structural Analysis
1. Universal Application:
   - सर्वत्र (sarvatra) - everywhere
   - देहजीवितादि (dehajīvitādi) - body, life, etc.
   - Complete scope

2. Progressive Freedom:
   - From attachment
   - From reactions
   - To established wisdom
