taxonomy of citta

pratyaya          representation
bija              genus               asana
pratyaksha        perception          pranayama
pratya            sensation           pratyahara
jnana             cognition           dharana
pratibha          intuition           dhyana
samadhi           concept             samadhi
sabija            class               specific concept
nirbija           category            pure concept
prajna            concept of categories  idea

that's a linearization of

bija,sabija is genus,species
pratibha is either pratyahara or pratyaksha    intuition
samadhi is either sabija or nirbija            concept
prajna is samadhi of nirbija samadhis          reason
